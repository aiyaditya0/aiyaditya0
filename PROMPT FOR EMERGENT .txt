PROMPT FOR EMERGENT (Copy Everything)
Build a full-stack SaaS web application called AI Company OS.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                The idea is that a single founder can manage an entire business with a team of AI employees.
The system should support multiple AI departments that collaborate with each other, break down goals into tasks, perform research, generate outputs, and ask for approval before executing important actions.
This should be a production-quality application with both frontend and backend.
________________


CORE CONCEPT
The user acts as the Owner.
The Owner gives a high-level objective.
Example:
* Grow my business
* Find new clients
* Build a marketing strategy
* Launch a SaaS
* Generate content
* Research competitors
A CEO AI receives the goal.
The CEO AI breaks the goal into subtasks and delegates them to specialized AI employees.
Each AI employee works independently and sends updates back to the CEO.
The CEO combines the results and presents them to the Owner.
Sensitive actions require approval.
